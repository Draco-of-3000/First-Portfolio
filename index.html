WHY I DESIGN

Great design combines simplicity, empathy, and creativity. These are all values I strive for in my day-to-day life; whether I am cracking jokes with friends, freestyling to a trap beat, or brewing a cup of tea for my parents. Designing interfaces and digital experiences are simply manifestations of those values.
